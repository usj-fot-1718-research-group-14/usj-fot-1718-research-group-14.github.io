Revolutionizing Student Engagement Monitoring and Insights

Real-time Facial Expression Analysis

EngageSense utilizes advanced facial recognition technology to capture students' facial expressions during online classes. This enables us to assess their engagement levels based on emotional cues, providing valuable insights into their learning experience.

Heart Rate Monitoring

By seamlessly pairing with a smartwatch, EngageSense allows students to conveniently retrieve their heart rate data. This information is combined with facial expressions to provide a comprehensive assessment of their engagement and overall well-being, ensuring a holistic understanding of their learning environment.

Administrator Dashboard and Insights

EngageSense provides administrators with an intuitive dashboard. From there, they can effortlessly upload class links and modules, monitor overall engagement in real-time, and make data-driven decisions to optimize the learning environment. This centralized platform empowers administrators to effectively manage and enhance the online learning experience for students.
